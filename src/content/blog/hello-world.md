# Hello, World

This is the first post on my personal site. I've been meaning to build a proper space to write for a while — somewhere to collect thoughts on research, projects, and things I find interesting.

## Why a website?

A few reasons:

- **Writing clarifies thinking.** I want to write more about what I'm learning, especially in ML research. Explaining something forces you to understand it properly.
- **A portfolio that evolves.** LinkedIn and CVs are static. A personal site can grow and change as I work on new things.
- **Sharing is underrated.** Most of what I've learned came from people who took the time to write things down. I'd like to contribute to that.

## What to expect

I'll write about AI/ML research, blockchain, systems programming, and whatever else I find worth sharing. No fixed schedule — just whenever something is worth writing about.

If you're interested, check out my [work](/work) or the [reading list](/blog) for things I've found valuable.

More soon.
